Overdraft Fee Policy:
If your checking account balance goes below zero, an overdraft fee may be charged per transaction. Fees are capped at 3 overdraft charges per day. Deposits posted before cutoff may reduce overdraft impact.

Credit Card Interest Policy:
Credit card interest is charged when statement balances are not paid in full by the due date. Interest is calculated using average daily balance and the annual percentage rate (APR).

Payment Rules:
Minimum payments are due by 11:59 PM local time on the due date. Late payments may trigger a late fee and can increase your APR after repeated misses.

Dispute and Chargeback Rule:
Cardholders should report unauthorized charges within 60 days of statement date. Temporary credit may be issued while investigation is in progress.
